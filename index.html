Campus

I qualified for the Doon in January 2021

The Doon School is a boarding school for boys. It opened in 1935 fo Indian kids as the British did not let the Indians study. The campus has lots of activities and is the best school in India. At the campus the boys learn how to be leaders and good friends.



What The School Has Achieved

Number 1 School Of India

Doon has been credited with pioneering mountaineering in India, due to the accomplishments of masters such as R.L. Holdsworth, Jack Gibson and Gurdial Singh, and alumni like Nandu Jayal, who later became the founder principal of Himalayan Mountaineering Institute.
